How It Works

Simple, transparent, and compliant workflow.

  1. Submit a prep request — complete our intake form with shipment and prep details.
  2. Ship inventory to our facility — receiving address is provided after request approval.
  3. Receiving & inspection — boxes are logged, counted, and visually inspected upon arrival.
  4. Prep completed — all prep is completed according to Amazon FBA guidelines.
  5. Invoice & payment — invoice is issued based on completed services and storage time.
  6. Shipment sent to Amazon FBA — inventory is shipped after payment confirmation.
Important: Outbound shipments are released only after payment is received.

Services

Amazon FBA Prep Services

We provide Amazon FBA-compliant prep services to ensure your inventory meets all inbound requirements before being shipped to fulfillment centers.

  • FNSKU labeling
  • Polybagging
  • Bubble wrap
  • Bundling and sets
  • Carton prep
  • Expiration date labels
All prep work is performed according to current Amazon FBA guidelines.

Receiving & Inspection

All inbound shipments are received, logged, and visually inspected upon arrival at our facility.

  • Box receiving
  • Quantity verification
  • Visual inspection
  • Damage or issue reporting
We do not open sealed manufacturer packaging unless requested.

Temporary Storage

We offer short-term storage to support inventory processing and outbound shipment scheduling.

  • Short-term storage only
  • Storage is billed per box
  • Weekly or monthly billing options
  • Standard box size and weight limits apply
Storage is intended for temporary holding during prep and is not designed for long-term warehousing.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PrepNest NY LLC (“PrepNest NY”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our website and intake forms.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you submit an intake request:

  • Contact details (such as name, email address, phone number)
  • Business information (company name, seller type, marketplace)
  • Shipment estimates (number of boxes, total units)
  • Selected services (prep, receiving, storage)
  • Files you choose to upload (for example, labels or packing lists), if enabled
  • Technical data such as IP address and basic server logs for security and abuse prevention

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Review and respond to your intake request
  • Provide quotes and confirm scope of services
  • Coordinate receiving, prep, and storage workflows
  • Communicate about scheduling, requirements, and operational updates
  • Maintain internal records for operations, billing, and compliance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or lawful requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety and prevent misuse
  • Work with service providers strictly required to operate our business (such as hosting or email delivery), under confidentiality and limited-use obligations

4. Data Storage and Retention

Information submitted through our intake forms is stored on our server and retained only as long as reasonably necessary for operational processing, recordkeeping, and legal or compliance purposes. Data may be deleted or anonymized when it is no longer needed.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Choices

You may request to:

  • Access or update the information you submitted
  • Request deletion of your information, where legally and operationally feasible
  • Stop receiving non-essential communications

7. Cookies and Analytics

We may use minimal cookies or basic analytics to improve our website and prevent abuse. If analytics are used, they are applied in aggregate form and are not intended to identify you personally.

8. International Visitors

If you access our website from outside the United States, your information may be processed and stored in the United States.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for business users and are not directed to children under the age of 13.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, you may contact us at:
Email: support@joynhappiness.com
Company: PrepNest NY LLC, Brooklyn, NY

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

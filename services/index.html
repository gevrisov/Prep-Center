FAQ

Do you work only with Amazon FBA?

Yes. We provide services exclusively for Amazon FBA.

Are you affiliated with Amazon?

No. PrepNest NY is an independent prep center and is not affiliated with Amazon.

Do you offer long-term storage?

No. We offer temporary storage only, intended to support prep and outbound scheduling.

How is storage billed?

Storage is billed per box, with weekly or monthly billing.

What are the standard box limits?

Maximum 24" × 18" × 18" and 50 lbs per box. Non-standard boxes may be declined.

Who creates the shipment in Amazon Seller Central?

The client. You create shipment plans, FNSKU, carton details, and labels.

When do you ship to Amazon FBA?

Outbound shipments are released only after prep is completed and payment is received.

Do you accept hazardous goods (hazmat)?

No. Hazardous or dangerous goods are not accepted.

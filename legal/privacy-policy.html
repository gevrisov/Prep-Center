Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-04

This Privacy Policy explains how PrepNest NY LLC (“PrepNest NY”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our website and intake forms.

1) Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you submit an intake request:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone)
  • Business info (company name, seller type, marketplace)
  • Shipment estimates (number of boxes, total units)
  • Service selections (prep, receiving, storage)
  • Files you choose to upload (e.g., labels, packing lists) — if enabled
  • Technical data (IP address, basic server logs) for security and abuse prevention

2) How We Use Your Information

We use submitted information to:

  • Review and respond to your intake request
  • Provide quotes, confirm scope, and coordinate receiving/prep workflows
  • Communicate about scheduling, requirements, and operational updates
  • Maintain internal records for operations, billing, and compliance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access

3) Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations (lawful requests, court orders)
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety and prevent misuse
  • Work with service providers strictly required to operate our business (e.g., hosting, email delivery), under confidentiality and limited-use terms

4) Data Storage & Retention

Intake submissions are stored on our server and retained only as long as reasonably necessary for: operational processing, recordkeeping, and legal/compliance purposes. We may delete or anonymize data when it is no longer needed.

5) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6) Your Choices

You may request to:

  • Access or update your submitted information
  • Delete your information (when legally and operationally feasible)
  • Stop receiving non-essential communications

7) Cookies & Analytics

We may use minimal cookies or basic analytics to improve the website and prevent abuse. If analytics are enabled, they are used in aggregate and are not intended to identify you personally.

8) International Visitors

If you access our website from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the U.S.

9) Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for business users and are not directed to children under 13.

Pricing Overview

Pricing is based on shipment volume, prep requirements, and storage duration.

Prep & Receiving

  • Prep services are billed per unit
  • Receiving is billed per box
  • Special or non-standard requests are quoted individually

Storage

  • Storage is billed per box
  • Short-term storage only
  • Weekly or monthly billing
  • Standard box size and weight limits apply

Notes

Final pricing and approval depend on request details and operational requirements.

Minimum volume and minimum monthly charges apply.
We reserve the right to decline shipments that do not meet our operational requirements.
